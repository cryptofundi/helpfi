Frequently Asked Questions

What is HelpFi?

HelpFi is a community-driven crypto token that automatically donates 3% of every transaction to CryptoRelief to support healthcare innovation and relief efforts. An additional 2% goes to a marketing/operations wallet to sustain and grow the project.

Is HelpFi a profit-based token?

No. HelpFi is a social-impact token. It makes no profit guarantees. It is designed to contribute to real-world causes while optionally benefiting holders if the community grows organically.

Where can I buy HelpFi?

HelpFi is launched on the Base Blockchain. You can buy it on decentralized exchanges like BaseSwap or Dexscreener. Always verify the official contract address from our homepage before trading.

Is the smart contract verified?

Yes, the HelpFi contract is open-source and verified on BaseScan. You can view and audit it yourself. We believe in full transparency for every line of code.

Is CryptoRelief officially affiliated with HelpFi?

No, CryptoRelief is not officially affiliated with HelpFi. We use their publicly available donation address. We will update all communication if official affiliation is established in the future.

What happens when I buy HelpFi?

When you buy HelpFi for $100:

  • 3% ($3) goes directly to the CryptoRelief wallet.
  • 2% ($2) goes to HelpFi's marketing and operational wallet.
  • You receive 95% of the tokens.

Can I earn from promoting or working on HelpFi?

Yes. HelpFi is an open project. Contributors may be compensated transparently from the operations wallet (2% of transactions). Please ensure you declare any income earned according to your local tax laws.

Can anyone contribute to the project?

Absolutely! Developers, designers, educators, and marketers are welcome. Just reach out through our social channels or GitHub. We're fully community-led and open to meaningful contributions.

What is Crypto Fundi?

Crypto Fundi is our side initiative — a free and open crypto education platform. It helps beginners around the world learn blockchain fundamentals with easy-to-understand lessons and tools.

Is there any utility in the future if I hold long term?

Yes. While not guaranteed, we are exploring utilities like:

  • Governance votes
  • NFT drops
  • Early access to campaigns
  • Incentives for contributors and educators

I bought HelpFi and made a profit. How should I view this?

It’s a win-win. You supported a health initiative and made a gain. Report it as per your local tax rules. Just remember: HelpFi is designed for impact first, not speculation.

Why should I buy HelpFi instead of donating directly to CryptoRelief?

We encourage direct donations too! But HelpFi provides:

  • Continuous donations with every transaction
  • Community participation and optional upside
  • Education support via Crypto Fundi
  • Transparent on-chain giving

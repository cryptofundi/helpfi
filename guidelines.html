HelpFi Community Sharing Guidelines

Promote with Integrity – Stay Legal, Stay Purpose-Driven

Why Guidelines?

HelpFi is a decentralized project built to create real-world impact through transparent crypto donations. We are not an investment vehicle, fundraising platform, or meme token. To protect our mission and ensure compliance with global laws, it’s important to follow these sharing guidelines when promoting HelpFi.

✅ What You Can Say

  • ✅ HelpFi automatically donates 3% of every transaction to a public NGO wallet
  • ✅ It's a transparent, purpose-driven token – no central owner, no fundraising
  • ✅ HelpFi encourages ongoing impact through on-chain, verifiable giving
  • ✅ Highlight our cause: Public health, innovation, and transparency

❌ What You Should NOT Say

  • ❌ Don’t claim HelpFi will go "to the moon" or make people rich
  • ❌ Don’t suggest it’s an investment or has ROI
  • ❌ Don’t use hype words like "pump," "moonshot," or "100x"
  • ❌ Don’t promise any personal, financial, or token-based rewards
  • ❌ Don’t falsely claim HelpFi is officially partnered with any NGO or foundation

Important Notes

  • 🛡️ HelpFi is a community initiative – not controlled or funded by any centralized entity
  • 🔗 3% of all trades are automatically routed to the CryptoRelief wallet listed on cryptorelief.in
  • 💡 We are transparent by design – our contract is public, and all wallet flows are traceable

Need Help?

If you’re unsure whether a tweet or post is okay to share, just ask in our community or check this page again. Let’s build this ethically and safely – together.

Welcome to HelpFi

Crypto for social good. Every transaction supports real-world impact through donations to healthcare relief and free crypto education.

How It Works

HelpFi is a decentralized, community-powered crypto token designed for impact. With every transaction:

  • 3% is automatically donated to CryptoRelief
  • 2% goes toward community growth and development
  • 95% remains in your wallet — you hold value and help create change

Why Choose HelpFi?

  • Transparent on-chain donations to public health
  • Supports Crypto Fundi — free crypto education for everyone
  • No promises, no pump — just people helping people
  • Community-first: open-source, volunteer-led, and impact-driven

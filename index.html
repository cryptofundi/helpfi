What is HelpFi?

HelpFi is a crypto token created with one mission: to support real-world impact through decentralized funding. With every transaction, 3% is sent directly to CryptoRelief, helping them continue their incredible work in public health and biomedical innovation.

How It Works

  • 3% of every buy/sell: Auto-sent to CryptoRelief donation wallet
  • 2% of every buy/sell: Sent to HelpFi Operations Wallet to fund awareness and infrastructure
  • 100% Transparency: All on-chain, open-source contract, no central control

Wallet Naming Standard

We follow a clear and transparent wallet structure:

  • Donation Wallet: Funds automatically routed to the verified CryptoRelief wallet (currently: 0x68A99f89E475a078645f4BAC491360aFe255Dff1)
  • Operations Wallet: Used only for awareness, audits, upkeep, and infrastructure — not for personal gain

CryptoRelief → Blockchain For Impact (BFI)

CryptoRelief has seeded a new initiative, Blockchain For Impact (BFI), to carry forward its mission. While HelpFi routes funds to the CryptoRelief wallet listed publicly at cryptorelief.in, our values align with BFI’s focus on research, health, and innovation.

We will continue to monitor these transitions and maintain the integrity of donations. In the future, if a change is required, the Donation Wallet address may be updated (if contract ownership is retained).

Why Use HelpFi Instead of Donating Directly?

HelpFi encourages community-driven giving. Instead of one-time donations, token holders can support a cause while participating in a decentralized project built for transparency and ongoing social good.

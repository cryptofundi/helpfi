What is HelpFi?

HelpFi is a crypto token created with one mission: to support real-world impact through decentralized funding. With every transaction, 2% is sent directly to CryptoRelief, helping them continue their incredible work in public health and biomedical innovation.

How It Works

  • 2% of every buy/sell: Auto-sent to CryptoRelief wallet
  • 2% of every buy/sell: Sent to HelpFi Marketing/Admin wallet to fund growth
  • 100% Transparency: All on-chain, open-source contract, no central control

Why Use HelpFi Instead of Donating Directly?

HelpFi encourages **community-driven giving**. Instead of one-time donations, token holders can support a cause while participating in a decentralized project with the potential for growth, awareness, and long-term impact.
